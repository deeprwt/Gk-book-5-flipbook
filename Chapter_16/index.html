Oscar: The Academy Awards is the oldest worldwide entertainment award which started in 1930. It is now officially known as 'The Oscars'. It is a set of twenty-four awards for artistic and technical excellence in the American and international film industry.

Awards are presented every year to recognise achievements in the world of entertainment. These include music, films, documentaries, television, and more. Let us read about the most eminent awards.

BAFTA: Founded in 1947, the British Academy Film Awards, popularly known as BAFTA Film Awards, honour the best British and international contributions to the film industry.

IIFA: The International Indian Film Academy Awards commenced in 2000. It is a set of awards that honour both artistic and technical excellence of professionals in Bollywood, the second largest film industry.

National Film Awards: The National Film Awards are given by the Indian government. The president of India gives these awards at a ceremony in New Delhi every year. These awards are given for all categories for each region and language of the country.

Grammy: The Grammy Award was established in 1959 to honour the outstanding achievements in the English-language music industry. The award was originally known as 'Gramophone Awards.'
