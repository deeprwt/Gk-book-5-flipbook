Cave paintings of Ajanta and Ellora are evidence that the art of painting was present in India from the ancient times. There are many Indian painters who have received global recognition. Let us read and find out more about them.

Raja Ravi Varma was a renowned painter known for depicting scenes from epics such as Mahabharata and Ramayana. Some of his paintings are Shantanu and Matsyagandha and Portrait of a Lady.

Satish Gujral is a painter, graphic designer, muralist, and writer of the post independent era. Some of his paintings are Mourning en masse and Days of Glory.

MF Hussain, also known as the 'Picasso of India', was an internationally recognized Indian painter of the 20th century. Some of his paintings are Gaja Gamini and Horses.

Jamini Roy was recognized by the Indian government because of his contribution to arts. His most famous works include Bride and two companions and Gopini.

Amrita Sher-Gil is the only Indian who was elected as an associate of the Grand Salon in Paris in 1933. Her paintings have been declared as National Art Treasures by the Government of India. Some of her paintings include Young Girls and Hill Women.

Jatin Das is regarded as one of the most creative artists of India. His paintings mainly revolve around different categories of man-woman relationships. Some of his paintings include Radha Krishna and Ganpati.
