18. Banking matters

Savings Account and Fixed Deposit

A savings account is used to deposit money in order to save and earn interest. One can withdraw the money anytime through cheque, debit card, or internet banking.

A fixed deposit is where you deposit your money for a fixed period of time on which you receive a fixed rate of interest.

Activity 1: Making healthy food

There is a saying that 'a healthy mind lives in a healthy body.' For a healthy body, we need nutritious food. Sprouts are a rich source of protein that you can make yourself.

Materials required

one fistful of beans or dals

one glass jar/bowl with lid

vegetables like tomatoes, onions, cucumber, and lemon

one bowl

water

1.

Take the beans you want to sprout in a bowl.

2.

Wash the beans 3–4 times in water. Drain the water.

4.

Cover the jar with a lid and let it soak overnight.

3.

Put the beans in a jar and add water till it is one inch above the beans.

5.

In the morning, you will find that the beans are swollen.

6.

Remove all the water and close the lid. Make sure there is no water left.

8.

After 2–3 days, you will see beautiful sprouts in the bowl.

9.

You can now eat the sprouts by adding the vegetables of your choice like finely cut tomatoes, onions, or cucumber. Add salt, pepper, and lemon juice, for more flavour.

7.

Cover the jar and keep it like this for 2–3 days.

Your sprouts are now ready to eat. Enjoy your meal and share it with your family!
