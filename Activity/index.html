Activity 2: My city

A map is a symbolical and visual representation of a place, city, or country. It tells about its location, distance, and features. Follow the steps and make a map of your city.

Step 1: Paste the map of your city in the space provided.

Step 2: Mark the important monuments/places in the city and cross them with a brown pen.

Step 3: Make a red cross at the places where hospitals are located.

Step 4: Mark the location of your school in green colour.

Now, answer the following questions.

Give a one-line description of the places you have marked in brown (only three).

Find out the highest and lowest temperature of your city recorded last year and write them here.

State what is special about your city (it could be clothes/food/places).

Paste the map of your city here.
